Frequently asked questions

When can I join? ▾
The main sign-up period is during the Clubs & Societies Fair at the start of each semester — usually the second week of term. However, most societies accept new members year-round. Just contact them directly or show up to an event.
I have no experience — can I still join? ▾
Absolutely. All three featured clubs explicitly welcome beginners. The Film Society runs workshop nights for people who’ve never held a camera. The Coding Society has beginner-friendly study sessions. The Rugby team holds open trials for all ability levels.
How much time does it take up each week? ▾
It varies by club. Film Soc and Coding Soc typically run one evening event per week. Rugby training is twice weekly plus match days. You can always come along occasionally without committing to everything — most events are drop-in.
Can international students join? ▾
Yes — all registered UCD students are eligible to join any club or society regardless of nationality or programme. Clubs are a great way to meet people if you’re new to Dublin.
